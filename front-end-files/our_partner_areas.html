St.Francis VSA ARTS

St. Francis VSA ARTS (SF) is a school that provides education and therapy. While mostly for children who are hard of hearing, the school also has students that have other disabilities and some with none. The principal, Ma'am Becky, has over 25 years of experience with the sector and the school's therapy is centered around art. These artworks are sold by the students in weekend bazaars for the end goal of independence and self-sufficiency.
